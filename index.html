About me

Amit Amola

CS Postgrad @TCD

I am an enthusiastic and avid learner with knowledge and experience in using Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning techniques to create production-ready solutions. Currently, I am pursuing my second master's degree in Computer Science at Trinity College Dublin while working as a part-time Python instructor at Code First Girls.

In my previous role, I worked as a Senior AI Engineer at Accenture, where I was part of AI Labs and worked on the firm's Computer Vision related problems. I had the opportunity to contribute to the algorithm development team under Watch Tower, an AI Accelerator specializing in KPI and entity monitoring with real-time alerts for anomalies.

During my tenure at Accenture (Bi2i), I had the privilege to mentor over 200 analytics professionals in Python and provide guidance to 30+ resources in Computer Vision. It was a rewarding experience to share my knowledge and help others grow in their respective fields.

Education:

  • Masters in Computer Science, Trinity College Dublin
  • Masters in Operational Research, University of Delhi
  • Bachelors of Science in Mathematics (Hons.), University of Delhi

I am proud to have gained recognition on Stack Overflow with over 2000 reputations and expertise in top tags such as Python, OpenCV, Numpy, Pandas, and Matplotlib. On HackerRank, I achieved the world rank #1 in Python by completing all 115 challenges. Additionally, I actively contribute to open-source projects on GitHub and Kaggle Datasets, leveraging the power of collaboration to drive innovation and knowledge sharing.
